Girls In Tech Nigeria is a social enterprise and nonprofit organization for girls and women in technology in Nigeria. The organization was established to ensure that girls and women in Nigeria are fully represented in STEM fields and occupations, especially in information technology. 

Mission:
To empower women in technology in Nigeria.
    Goals and Objectives:
    Girls In Tech Nigeria believes that a mere representation in STEM fields and occupations is not enoughâ€“it takes real culture change to accomplish that through the following goals.
  1. Giving girls and women ownership in the technology space because ownership paves the way for learning opportunities for all girls and women.
  2. Offering mentorship and training because mentorship and training can and will prepare girls and women for the future in technology.
  3. Advocating for girls and women starts with leadership because making girls and women leaders in technology empower them to reject sustained abuses, subjugation, and forced and early marriages.
